Architecture

Built to be secure.

A bug in the wrong place can't touch your money.

L1
IntentValidator Checks your signature is valid and hasn't been used before. Never holds money.
no funds
L2
SolverVerifier Tracks which solvers are trustworthy. Bad actors lose their stake permanently.
no funds
L3
ExecutionSandbox Double-checks the solver's work before anything moves. If it's not good enough, the whole thing cancels.
no funds
L4
SettlementFinalizer The only part that moves money. Intentionally tiny — less code means less that can go wrong.
moves funds
L5
DisputeEscrow Solver fees are held for 10 minutes so anyone can challenge a bad fill. Then they're released automatically.
optimistic
Security model

Only one part of the protocol ever touches your money. Everything else — the verification, the solvers, the dispute system — is completely separate. A problem in any of those can't reach your funds.
