01 — How it works

Simple for users.
Powerful under the hood.

No bridge navigation. No chain selection. No failed transactions.

01 — USER
Sign an intent
Tell INTNT what you want — swap this, earn yield, buy if price drops. Sign it with your wallet. No gas paid, no transaction sent. Just a signed message.
02 — SOLVERS
Compete to fill it
A network of registered solvers races to find the best execution path across every DEX, bridge, and liquidity source. Whoever gives you the best outcome wins the fee.
03 — PROTOCOL
Enforce the outcome
The 5-layer settlement engine verifies the solver's solution atomically before a single dollar moves. Fall short of your terms? Full revert. You lose nothing.
04 — YOU
Got exactly what you wanted
Output lands in your wallet. No intermediate steps, no stuck tokens, no surprises. The protocol handled every step between your intent and its execution.

02 — Architecture

Built so a bug
can't drain your funds.

Financial logic lives in one minimal contract. Everything else is isolated.

LAYER 01
Intent validator
Verifies your EIP-712 signature, checks the deadline, and consumes the nonce to prevent replay attacks. No funds ever enter this contract.
NO FUNDS
LAYER 02
Solver verifier
Checks that the solver is registered and has staked . Bad actors are slashed automatically — stake burned, solver deactivated.
NO FUNDS
LAYER 03
Execution sandbox
Simulates the solver's execution path and measures the output before any money moves. If you won't get at least what you asked for, it reverts.
NO FUNDS
LAYER 04
Settlement
The only contract that moves funds — intentionally under 100 lines. Calls layers 1→2→3 in sequence, then executes the transfer atomically.
FUNDS HERE
LAYER 05
Dispute escrow
Solver fees sit in escrow for 10 minutes. You can challenge fraud within that window. After it closes, fees release automatically.
OPTIMISTIC
